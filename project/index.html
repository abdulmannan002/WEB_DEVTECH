Frustration and Exhaustion is key

Welcome to CodeFatigue

This website isn't just a project—it's the result of countless hours of frustration, exhaustion, and stubborn persistence. Every line of code carries a story of bugs that refused to be fixed, designs that didn’t align, and moments where quitting felt easier than continuing.

CodeFatigue is more than a name; it’s a reflection of the struggles every developer faces. It’s about embracing the chaos, the late nights, and the overwhelming sense of “Why won’t this work?” while pushing forward anyway.

Here, frustration and exhaustion aren’t just obstacles—they’re the driving force behind creativity, learning, and resilience. If you’ve ever battled with code, stared blankly at an error message, or felt the burnout creeping in, you’re not alone. This is a space that understands the journey and celebrates the triumph of not giving up.

Welcome to the world where frustration meets creation. Welcome to CodeFatigue.
